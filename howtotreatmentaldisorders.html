How To Treat Mental Disorders

Mental disorders can be a menace to an individual, family, community, and society at large if not diagnosed and treated early. There are different ways to treat mental disorders including;

  • Psychotherapy
  • Self Help Plans
  • Support from family and friends
  • Medical prescriptions
  • Hospitalization
  • Behavioural therapy
